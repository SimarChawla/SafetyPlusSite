About Us

Feel Safe About Safety & Compliance

Our team of experienced professionals at Safety Plus is dedicated to providing the highest quality of service for our clients’ trucking safety and compliance needs. Established in 2012, Safety Plus has served over 250 companies across North America. Safety Plus is a one stop destination for all your safety and compliance needs.

Our mission is to create long-lasting partnerships with our clients and work together to ensure the success of their firms. Honesty, integrity, and commitment to our customers, are our company’s founding values and we stand by these in everything that we do at Safety Plus.

Contact us today to see how our team can meet your safety and compliance needs.

Services

Safety Plus can help you stay on top of your company’s safety and compliance obligations, both in Canada and the US. Our personnel are trained and experienced and will help you make this process as streamlined as possible.

  • C-TPAT: Our consultants will help you through the requirements and process of obtaining C-TPAT certification.

  • Customs Self-Assessment (CSA) Application: Safety Plus will support you through all parts of the CSA application process.

  • PIP: Our consultants will help guide you through the requirements and process of obtaining PIP certification.

  • SmartWay: Safety Plus will help you in getting the SmartWay environmental certification.

Authorities and Permits

Safety Plus can help in guiding you through the process for obtaining all relevant authorities and permits in Canada and the US, as well as making sure they are up to date. We can help you with the following:

CVOR Registration

DOT Registration

MC # Registration

Oregon Weight Distance Tax Registration

New York Highway Use Tax Registration

Kentucky Weight Distance Tax Registration

New Mexico Weight Distance Tax Registration

  • Fuel and Weight Distance Tax Filing

    We can handle filing your quarterly IFTA Fuel Tax, as well as your NY, KY, and Oregon Weight Distance Taxes.

  • Drug Testing

    Safety Plus employs several certified and experienced DOT Specimen Collectors and Breath Alcohol Technicians for random and pre-employment drug tests.
